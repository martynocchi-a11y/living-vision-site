Leadership Is Inconvenient

The easy choice now always has a long-term cost. Always.

There are two payment plans for leadership. Pay now — with discomfort, vulnerability, and change. Or defer — and pay later in burnout, regret, and the quiet cost of years that passed while you were waiting for the right moment.

The deferred cost is always multiplied. The hard conversation you didn't have. The presence you kept meaning to give. The version of yourself you kept meaning to become.

Leadership means choosing to pay the inconvenient cost now so the people following you don't pay it later.
